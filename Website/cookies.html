New york cookies

Some clasic new york style cookies! A recipe for 8 cookies aprox.

Ingredients

  • 160 gr of butter
  • 95 gr of brown sugar
  • 45 gr of regular sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 300 gr of flour
  • 4 gr of cornstarch
  • 3 gr of baking powder
  • 2 gr of baking soda
  • 3 ml of vanilla extract
  • as many chocolate chips as you like

Steps

  1. Mix the butter and both sugars until the mixture is kinda creamy
  2. Add the egg and the vanilla, mix
  3. Add the dry ingredients and chocolate chips
  4. Make small cookies (they should look like spheres) of 60 gr aprox. and put them in the fridge for like 3 hours
  5. Preheat the oven at 170* celcius
  6. Put the cookies in the oven for 25 minutes
  7. Get them out of the oven
  8. Enjoy!
