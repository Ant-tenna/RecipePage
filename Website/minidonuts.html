Mini donuts

Like donuts, but mini. This recipe is for like 50 minidonuts aprox.

Ingredients

  • 180 gr of regular sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 400 gr of flour
  • 300 ml of milk
  • 6 gr of baking powder
  • 60 ml of oil
  • 3 ml of vanilla extract
  • any toppings youll like!

Steps

  1. Mix the egg and sugar until the mixture is fluffy
  2. Add the oil and vanilla
  3. pour the milk in small amounts
  4. add the dry ingredients, also in small amounts
  5. Preheat the mini donuts machine
  6. Put the dough in the mini donuts machine
  7. wait 1 minute and flip them over to wait 1 minute more
  8. put the toppings youll like
  9. enjoy!
